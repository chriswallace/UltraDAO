Twelve Days of CryptoArt

A blind mint experience featuring top established and emerging artists in the cryptoart scene.

Twelve days.
Twelve mysterious artworks.
One choice.

Every day, you are faced with a decision. Mint the artwork of the day or hold what you already have.

There is only one rule: you can't go back. Once you've minted the day's artwork, you cannot revert to a previous piece.

However, it is possible to mint every artwork every single day if you so choose. At the end, we reveal the names of the artists and share details about each piece.
