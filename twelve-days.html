Twelve Days of CryptoArt

Every day a mysterious new artwork awaits you.
Choose wisely, for you cannot go back.

Introducing a blind mint digital art experience featuring top established and emerging artists in the cryptoart scene.

Every day, you're faced with a decision to mint or hold. There is only one rule: you can't go back. When you mint the art of the day, you cannot revert to a previous piece.

At the end of twelve days, we lock the art in and reveal the artists.

How to get a Twelve Days Mint Pass

All ULTRAPASS holders will be airdropped a free Twelve Days Mint Pass to participate in the event. Once mint passes are available on secondary, you may purchase from royalty-honoring marketplaces like OpenSea. 100% of secondary royalties of mint passes and Twelve Days artwork will go directly to participating artists.

Not yet an ULTRAPASS holder?

We're auctioning eight ULTRAPASS tokens which all come with one free Twelve Days Mint Pass, airdropped within 24 hours of auction closing. All auctions end on December 15th.
