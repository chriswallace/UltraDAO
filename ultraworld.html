Announcing ULTRAWORLD Exhibition 001: Blockchain Origins

ULTRAWORLD Exhibition 001 is the inaugural showcase event of the UltraDAO Foundation World. This exhibition is set to feature an eclectic array of cryptoart from top international artists.

Slated for November 2023, this exhibition seeks to cast a spotlight on a kaleidoscope of artistic styles, origins, and backgrounds. We're on a quest for avant-garde art pieces that embody unique narratives and enigmatic expressions. It’s not merely about displaying art; it’s about sharing the riveting stories and the profound ideologies that birthed them. This is a call to all visionary artists to come forth and share their oeuvre with the world.

Submission Guidelines

Artists are invited to submit one artwork with a detailed description and/or background on the piece. Submissions should be in a digital format and must not have been minted or sold previously. Pricing is up to you, however, we will be happy to make our own recommendation as well.

Next steps

We will email all selected artists no later than two weeks after the submission deadline. At this point, we will ask you to mint your piece to Foundation ahead of the exhibition.

Key details

Submission Deadline: 11am CST, November 2nd, 2023

Exhibition Launch: November 27th, 2023

Exhibition Venue: UltraDAO Digital Gallery, accessible online worldwide

Artwork Sale Split: 80% to the artist, 20% to UltraDAO
