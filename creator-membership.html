Creator Membership

Minting July 14th

The premier membership for digital artists and web3 creators.

UltraDAO is a future-thinking creative arts organization bringing together artists, developers, and entrepreneurs to create unique entertainment media, decentralized brands, and NFT projects. The UltraDAO Creator Membership is a brand-new, exclusive access pass to join the UltraDAO creator community.

Drop Details

  • Supply: 20,000 NFTs on the Ethereum blockchain
  • Mint price: 0.1eth

Schedule

  • Mint opens July 14th at 5:15pm Eastern Daylight Time
  • Minters have 48 hours to mint from allowlist
  • Public mint opens July 16th at 5:15pm Eastern Daylight Time

Allowlist

  • 15,000 reserved mints for collab Premint allowlist
  • ~3,000 free mints reserved for existing UltraDAO members
  • ~1,000 free mints reserved for Woodies holders allowlist who registered through Premint
  • 500 free mints reserved for scholarships for under-represented creators
  • 328 free mints reserved for 333 Club members
  • 222 free mints reserved for Street Beast holders

Member Benefits

  • Creator Directory: Allow members to connect with one another and companies to find creative talent in the web3 space.
  • Blockchain tools: Get access to custom blockchain tools for creators, powered by Props.
  • Educational resources: Learn from established players in the space, including long-time UltraDAO members who have launched successful projects.
  • Weekly Creator Chats: Present your ideas and get instant feedback from other creators and mentors in web3.
  • Creator Fund: Submit a project proposal, build a team, and get funded to execute on your ideas.
  • Governance: Vote on which projects to green-light through regular governance cycles for the Creator Fund.

Introducing the Creator Fund.

The Creator Fund is our treasury where creators submit projects and members vote on which ones to green-light. Through our decentralized governance system, creator members hold the power to fund other creator-led projects. A portion of the Creator Membership mint will be dedicated to the fund. We will be working with corporate sponsors to design additional funds for specific types of projects (for example: documentaries, generative art projects, new intellectual property, etc.).

UltraDAO members have built game-changing brands leveraging relationships gained and expertise of other members.

UltraDAO's most successful project to date is Woodies which released its genesis NFT collection in September 2021.

The project sold 10,000 mint passes for ~$2.4 million USD in just 30 minutes at mint and is still building new IP, lore, and community. The team also donated over $250,000 to plant one million trees with Trees for the Future.
