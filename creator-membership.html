Creator Membership

Coming July 14th

The premier membership for digital artists and web3 creators.

UltraDAO is a future-thinking creative arts organization bringing together artists, developers, and entrepreneurs to create unique entertainment media, decentralized brands, and NFT projects. The UltraDAO Creator Membership is a brand-new, exclusive access pass to join the UltraDAO creator community.

Members receive:

  • Access to Discord community of creators and collectors
  • Inclusion in UltraDAO creator directory
  • Governance/voting rights for UltraDAO Creator Fund
  • Access to educational resources on NFTs and web3 topics
  • Access to exclusive tools and services
  • Discounts on products and services
  • Exclusive IRL and virtual events

Introducing the Creator Fund.

The Creator Fund is our treasury where creators submit projects and members vote on which ones to green-light. Through our unique decentralized governance system, creators hold the power to fund other creator-led projects. A portion of the Creator Membership mint will be dedicated to the fund. We will be working with corporate sponsors to design additional funds for specific types of projects (for example: documentaries, generative art projects, new intellectual property, etc.).

UltraDAO members have built game-changing brands leveraging relationships gained and expertise of other members.

UltraDAO's most successful project to date is Woodies which released its genesis NFT collection in September 2021.

The project sold 10,000 mint passes for ~$2.4 million USD in just 30 minutes at mint and is still building new IP, lore, and community. The team also donated over $250,000 to plant one million trees with Trees for the Future.
